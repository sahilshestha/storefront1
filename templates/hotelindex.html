Our Story

A Sanctuary Built for the Thoughtful Traveller

Opened in 2001 on the northeastern shore of Phewa Lake, Aurum was conceived as a counterpoint to bustle — a place where architecture defers to landscape, and service anticipates without intruding.

Every wing faces the Annapurna range. Every room uses locally quarried stone and hand-loomed textiles. And our kitchen grows 60% of what it serves in the walled garden behind the main lodge.
